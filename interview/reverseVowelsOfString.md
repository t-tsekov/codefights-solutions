<code>reverseVowelsOfString(s) = "cidefoghts"</code>;</li>

<code>reverseVowelsOfString(s) = "UOaIye"</code>.</li>

<ul>
<li><strong>[time limit] 4000ms (py3)</strong></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>[input] string s</strong></p>

<li>
<p><strong>[output] string</strong></p>
